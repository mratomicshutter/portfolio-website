Pixels, Passion, and Frames: My Creative Journey Unveiled

After earning my Bachelor's in Web Development, I embarked on a creative odyssey. It all began in 2010, a camera in hand and a thirst for storytelling. Photography became my canvas, and every click was a stroke of artistic expression. Soon, graphic design beckoned, introducing me to a world where ideas took shape through Adobe's vibrant palette. As I honed my skills in design, videography called out to me, inviting exploration into the realm of moving frames and narratives. My journey has been a fusion of code, captivating visuals, and dynamic storytelling‐an exhilarating creative adventure through life.
